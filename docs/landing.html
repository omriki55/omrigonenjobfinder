Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

One GitHub repo does the work of five tools — scanning, scoring, coaching, and supporting you throughout your search.

🔍
Auto-scans 3× daily

GitHub Actions scrapes your target companies' ATS boards every 8 hours and adds fresh openings to your tracker automatically.

🎯
AI fit scoring 1–10

Claude reads each job description against your profile and scores the match, so you focus energy where it counts most.

🏢
Company dossier — one click

Walk into every interview knowing the company's product, culture, funding, and growth story — generated on demand.

🎙️
Voice interview practice

Realistic AI-powered mock interviews tailored to each role. Get sharp answers ready before the real call.

💙
Emotional support chat

Job searching is hard. A built-in empathetic chat helps you process rejections and stay motivated through the journey.

🔗
Add any job by URL

Paste a LinkedIn or company careers link and the AI pulls out the details, scores the fit, and adds it to your pipeline.
